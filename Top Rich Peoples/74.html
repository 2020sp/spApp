Carlos Alberto Sicupira

Carlos Alberto Sicupira (born 1948) is a Brazilian businessman, a partner in 3G Capital, which owns or has major stakes in Burger King, H.J. Heinz Company, Anheuser-Busch InBev and Lojas Americanas.

Early life

Carlos Alberto Sicupira was born in Brazil. He has a degree from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro.

Career

According to Forbes, he has an estimated net worth of $12.7 billion in February 2017.

Personal life

He is married and has three children.
